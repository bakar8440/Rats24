Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services and process

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on size and complexity. Small projects may take 2-3 months, while large commercial projects can take 12-18 months. We provide detailed timelines during the planning phase.

Do you provide free consultations?

Yes, we offer free initial consultations to discuss your project requirements, budget, and timeline. This helps us understand your vision and provide accurate estimates.

What areas do you serve?

We serve the entire metropolitan area and surrounding regions within a 100-mile radius. Contact us to confirm if we cover your specific location.

Do you handle permits and regulations?

Yes, we handle all necessary permits, zoning requirements, and building code compliance. Our team ensures your project meets all local and state regulations.

What warranty do you provide?

We provide comprehensive warranties on all our work. Structural elements are covered for 10 years, while finishes and systems have 1-5 year warranties depending on the component.

Can you work with my existing architect?

Absolutely! We work with external architects and designers. We can also provide in-house design services if needed. Our goal is to bring your vision to life regardless of who designed it.

What types of technical services do you offer?

We provide a wide range of technical services including electrical, mechanical, and general maintenance for residential, commercial, and industrial clients.

Do you handle tiles fixing for both homes and businesses?

Yes, our tiles fixing services cover both residential and commercial spaces, ensuring professional installation and finishing for floors, walls, kitchens, and bathrooms.

Can you provide custom welding and fabrication solutions?

Absolutely! We offer custom welding and fabrication for structural steel, decorative metalwork, and on-site repairs tailored to your project needs.

What does your renovation service include?

Our renovation services include home and office remodeling, painting, finishing, and structural repairs to modernize and upgrade your property.

How do you manage garbage maintenance?

We provide efficient waste management solutions, including scheduled pickup, disposal, and eco-friendly recycling for residential and commercial clients.

Do you offer plumbing and painting for large projects?

Yes, we handle plumbing and painting for projects of all sizes, from single homes to large commercial buildings, ensuring quality and durability.

About Rats24

Building excellence through innovation, quality, and dedication since 2025

Our Story

We are a collection of multi-technical and engineering services based in Dubai. We continuously strive to deliver the best service possible.

We recruit laborers from all around the world, primarily India, to acquire the best outcomes possible from them.

We think that learning never stops, therefore we pair our staff with the best educators in their industries.

We mostly work with tiles, welding and fabrication, parking shied, wallpaper, plumbing, and villa renovations, as well as painting contracts. We also undertake garbage maintenance automation.

We have three distinct departments: technical services, engineering work, and garbage maintenance.
